Hello World!

Welcome to the portfolio! My name is Ryan. To explain why this site exists, you may need some background.......

There used to be an operating system called Windows 98. This OS thrived around the time I was studying Philosophy in college. For obvious reasons, I needed a device to assist with my studies. So, I got myself a computer! And then it happened............I found the web.......

I was captivated with this technology that could connect people from different sides of the world. A program that could bring knowledge to and education to the entire world! And so, it began. I started teaching myself everything I could about this fascinating technology. Learning how to program it, and of course break it! And this continued for decades after. Self-teaching multiple languages including HTML, JavaScript, CSS, SQL, VBS (Classic ASP) and others. And then it happened. While working in a non tech role, I was asked if I could help with some techy things! What was once just a passion and hobby, turned into a possible career path. I currently work as a SAFe Product Owner and would love to transition to a full Product Development role. So why are you here and why would you consider me for an opportunity? Because after decades, I still have just as much passion for the field as I did when I wrote my first Hello World Program!
